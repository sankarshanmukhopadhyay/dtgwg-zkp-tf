# Editorial review checklist

- [ ] Status and normative status are explicit.
- [ ] The intended audience and scope are stated.
- [ ] Requirements, recommendations, examples and open issues are distinguishable.
- [ ] Every predicate states what it does and does not establish.
- [ ] Every privacy claim identifies adversary, context and horizon.
- [ ] Authority, delegation, enforcement, revocation and evidence are explicit.
- [ ] Failure behavior and degraded paths are documented.
- [ ] Terms and capitalization follow the controlled terminology.
- [ ] Diagrams have textual interpretations.
- [ ] Local links and anchors resolve.
- [ ] Scenarios, ADRs and conformance tests are traceable.
- [ ] No placeholder or future-work marker is presented as completed guidance.
