# Implementer checklist

## Authority and semantics

- [ ] Every accepted policy has an authority and version.
- [ ] Predicate positive and negative meanings are implemented consistently.
- [ ] Holder binding, delegation and transaction authorization are separate checks.
- [ ] Registry publication, recognition and reliance are separate decisions.

## Privacy and lifecycle

- [ ] Privacy claims name adversary, context and horizon.
- [ ] Metadata and error behavior are included in correlation analysis.
- [ ] Expiry, suspension, revocation, recovery and migration are deterministic.
- [ ] Offline and degraded paths do not silently weaken assurance.

## Evidence and conformance

- [ ] Decision receipts identify request, policy and governed state versions.
- [ ] Positive and negative conformance tests pass for the claimed level.
- [ ] Fixture digests, environment and exceptions are packaged.
- [ ] Accessibility and redress paths are documented and tested.
