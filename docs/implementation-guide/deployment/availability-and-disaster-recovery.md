# Availability and disaster recovery

Define service objectives for issuance, proving, verification, registry and evidence services. Recovery tests cover key access, policy versions, registry state, revocations, uniqueness state, delegation restrictions and evidence integrity.

Restoration shall not resurrect revoked credentials, expired policies or superseded authority state. Degraded modes are pre-authorized and produce explicit decision evidence.
