# Wallet compromise and recovery runbook

Recovery re-establishes holder control without resetting revocation, scoped uniqueness or delegation restrictions. Revoke compromised keys, invalidate active delegations as policy requires, rebind credentials through issuer-approved processes and preserve user redress evidence.
